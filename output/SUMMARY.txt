   4. Wanted level scaling is dynamic (1.1× to 60× multiplier range)

   7. Player agency is high (variable picks per window)

⚠️  WHAT NEEDS FIXING:
   1. Cautious Turtle is OVERPOWERED ($231k avg, 98% win)
      → Reduce holster win values by 5-10%
      → Add notoriety scaling to holster picks
   
   2. Aggro Gambler and Comeback Grinder are STRUGGLING
      → Buff Aggro Gambler: +5% base accuracy on confidence-3
      → Redesign Comeback Grinder: reduce skip costs, add desperation boost
   
   3. The Optimizer underperforms vs expectations
      → Increase holster frequency at high wanted levels
      → Test confidence drop curve
   
   4. Skip Burner bleeds balance on skip costs
      → Reduce cost scaling from 2.0× to 1.5×
      → OR add skip-specialist iron

================================================================================
30-MIN vs 120-MIN WINDOWS: COMPARISON
================================================================================

30-MINUTE WINDOWS (NEW):
✅ Higher engagement (checks app 3-5× per day)
✅ Shorter decision windows (lower commitment per pick)
✅ More frequent rewards (irons, balance updates)
✅ Higher volatility feel (noisier price action)
✅ Holster-friendly (defensive play rewarded)
✅ Mobile-optimized engagement pattern

TRADE-OFFS:
⚠️  Lower baseline survival (more churn)
⚠️  Higher variance in outcomes
⚠️  Directional picks harder to predict

VERDICT: 30-min windows are BETTER for mobile engagement, iron progression,
and keeping players invested. The higher volatility is INTENTIONAL GAME DESIGN.
