# Iron Catalog — 75 Irons for Bounty Hunter

Inspired by Balatro's joker system. Irons are passive modifiers collected after each round (pick 1 of 3). They stack in limited chamber slots and define your "build."

**Game context**: RISE/FALL (directional), HOLD (holster), SKIP. Confidence: Draw (1), Quick Draw (2), Dead Eye (3). Wanted level multiplier scales scoring. Notoriety determines level-ups/downs. Ante deducted each round.
