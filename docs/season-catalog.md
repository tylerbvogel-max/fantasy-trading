# Season Catalog

40 themed seasons designed to rotate throughout the year. Each exposes players to a different slice of the market, teaching sector dynamics, market concepts, and how real-world events move stock prices.

Seasons are grouped into four categories:
- **Sector Deep Dives** — Focus on a single industry
- **Thematic** — Cross-sector themes tied to a concept or trend
- **Event-Driven** — Timed to real-world market events
- **Educational Concept** — Designed to teach a specific investing principle

## Event-Driven Seasons

### 30. Magnificent 7 Earnings Showdown
**Stocks**: AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, NVDA, META, TSLA
**Timing**: Late January or late July (earnings season peaks)
**Duration**: 2-3 weeks around mega-cap earnings
**Teaches**: Earnings expectations vs reality, market concentration risk, how guidance moves stocks more than results

### 31. IPO Class
**Stocks**: The most notable IPOs from the current or recent year (updated each cycle)
**Timing**: Q4 (reviewing the year's IPOs)
**Teaches**: IPO pricing, lockup period expiration, why most IPOs underperform long-term

### 32. Tax Loss Harvesting
**Stocks**: The year's worst performers from the S&P 500 (curated each December)
**Timing**: December
**Teaches**: Tax loss harvesting strategy, January effect, contrarian investing

## Season Rotation Strategy

For year-round engagement, rotate 3-4 seasons simultaneously:

| Slot | Purpose | Duration | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Evergreen** | Always running, open to all | Continuous or quarterly | S&P 500 Challenge, Dividend Aristocrats |
| **Sector spotlight** | Deep dive into one industry | 4-6 weeks | Semiconductors, Big Banks, Oil & Gas |
| **Thematic** | Cross-sector trend | 4-6 weeks | AI Arms Race, Green Energy, Fintech |
| **Event sprint** | Tied to calendar event | 1-3 weeks | Mag 7 Earnings, Black Friday, Super Bowl |

This ensures players are always exposed to something new while having a stable season to fall back on. Over a full year, players would cycle through 20+ different sectors and concepts.

## Implementation Notes

- Each season references stocks by symbol from `stocks_master` (24K+ catalog already populated)
- `Season.allowed_stocks` (JSONB array) holds the curated symbol list per season
- Season creation is admin-only via `POST /admin/seasons`
- Stock lists above are starting points — should be reviewed and updated each time a season runs
- Some event-driven seasons (IPO Class, Tax Loss Harvesting, Election Year) need their stock lists refreshed each cycle
- The `Season.mode` field (classroom/league/arena) is independent of the theme — any theme can run in any mode
